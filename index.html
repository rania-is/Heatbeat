Join the Study

• Age between 18-55 years

• Living in Austria

• Regularly exercise outdoors (minimum 150 minutes/week)

• No cardiovascular conditions

• Own a smartphone (iOS or Android)

• Wear provided activity trackers during outdoor exercise

• Complete brief daily surveys about your exercise experience

• Continue your regular outdoor exercise routine

• Share basic health and demographic information

The study runs for one month (August 2025). You'll be asked to:

• Initial setup meeting (30 minutes)

• Daily activity tracking (during your regular exercise)

• Brief daily surveys (5 minutes)

• Final wrap-up meeting (30 minutes)

• €100 compensation upon study completion

• Personalized exercise and health insights report

• Free use of advanced fitness tracking devices during the study

• Contributing to important climate and health research

• All data is anonymized and stored securely

• Used only for research purposes

• Compliant with GDPR regulations

• You can request your data deletion at any time

• You'll receive a copy of your personal data
